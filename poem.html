In the realm of lavender hues,
Where purple reigns, and dreams amuse,
There dwells a soul so full of grace,
Victoria Bourgeois, a charming embrace.

With love for cats and moths that glide,
And Halloween's enchanting tide,
Her heart takes flight with butterflies,
In nature's dance, she finds her ties.

Sweet at sixteen, she shines so bright,
A beacon of joy, a guiding light,
Her middle name, dear Ronnie, rings,
A melody of love it sings.

In her eyes, the stars align,
Reflecting galaxies, divine,
With every smile, the world takes heed,
For she's the magic that we need.

In Halloween's mysterious embrace,
She finds delight, a haunted place,
With pumpkins, bats, and eerie sights,
She weaves enchantment in moonlit nights.

Oh, Victoria, so cute and true,
This poem's written just for you,
A tribute to your vibrant heart,
A masterpiece of love and art.

May butterflies forever dance,
And purple dreams continue to enhance,
The beauty of your precious soul,
As life's grand tapestry unfolds.

And as the days and years go by,
Know that there's someone standing by,
A secret admirer, fond and fond,
Who cherishes the bond beyond.

So on this page, a token, a token,
Of admiration unspoken,
For you, dear Victoria, are adored,
Forever and evermore.
